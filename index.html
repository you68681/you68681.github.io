Chao Lei


Hi there! 👋 I’m a PhD Candidate in the AI and Autonomy Lab at The University of Melbourne, under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Nir Lipovetzky, and Assoc. Prof. Kris Ehinger

My research focuses on AI planning, its application in large language models (LLMs), and LLM reasoning.

Feel free to contact me in case you have interest or questions regarding my work. 🤝
